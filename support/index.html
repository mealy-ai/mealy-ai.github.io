Support & contact

Mealy is a calm, supportive nutrition and wellness app. If anything is not working, or you have a question or feedback, we are happy to help.

Email us: mealy.support@gmail.com

We usually reply within 2 business days. Please include your device model and iOS version so we can help faster.

Common questions

  • Do I need an account? No. Mealy works right away without signing in. Signing in with Apple or email is optional and syncs your data across devices.
  • How do I delete my account and data? In the app, open the You tab and tap Delete account. This permanently removes your account and all your data.
  • How is my data handled? See our Privacy Policy. Chat messages and meal photos are processed by our AI partners (Google Gemini / OpenAI) to generate replies; nothing is used for advertising.
  • Is Mealy medical advice? No. Mealy offers general wellness guidance and is not a substitute for professional medical advice. See our Terms of Service.
